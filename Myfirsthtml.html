Steps to make a sandwich

  1. Get ingredients: bread, peanut butter, jelly, knife and place on counter
  2. Open peanut butter jar and use knife to spread 1 cm thick on on piece of bread
  3. Open jelly jar and use knife to spread 1 cm thick on other piece of bread
  4. Place bread together with the peanutbutter side and jelly side touching
  5. Eat and Enjoy!
